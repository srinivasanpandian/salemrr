Our Legacy

Serving Authentic Salem RR Biriyani Since 1998

Welcome to Salem RR Biriyani, where tradition meets taste in every aromatic grain. Our journey began in Salem, Tamil Nadu, with a commitment to preserve the authentic taste of traditional biriyani.

We take pride in our secret blend of spices, premium long-grain basmati rice, and time-honored cooking techniques that have been passed down through generations.
